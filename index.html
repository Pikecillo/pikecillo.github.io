About me

I'm a Software Engineer. Since March 2014, I work at HERE (formerly known as Nokia Maps, now an independent company) rendering beautiful maps.

In 2007, I got a degree in Systems Engineering from Universidad de Los Andes. In 2012, I got a MS in Computer Science from the University of Houston . There I worked as a Research Assistant at the Computer Graphics and Interactive Media Lab.

I am originally from a town nicknamed City of Gentlemen (Mérida, Venezuela). I lived for half a decade in a town nicknamed Space City (Houston, Texas). Since 2014 I live in a town which Tangerine Dream nicknamed Bear City (Berlin, Germany).
